Nature

This exhibition explores themes of nature within abstract art. Whether we want to depict its mystique or deny its structure, any manifestation of that energy flows in our artistic direction. An integral part of that energy is seen as present and ongoing. Our new artwork displays the ecological capacity to emerge as fully real, through each step in its natural unfolding, or rather at its slow-motion disappearance, through itself.
