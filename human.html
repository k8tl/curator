Humanity

Inspired by abstract forms and figures, this exhibition focuses on what it means to exist. How is a personal “history” woven into everyday experience, of real things, which by nature seem and work better than ever? And why do people today use photos and maps of other people to document the relationship between a single event, a daily event, a different world?
